# Objects
- player: white rectangle
- ground: green blocks
- walls: brown blocks
- spikes: red blocks
- platforms: grey blocks

# Behaviour
- player can jump when standing on ground or platforms
- player dies and respawns if they touch spikes or fall out of bounds

# Controls
- use left and right arrow keys to move horizontally
- press c to jump
- when colliding with a wall, hold z to grab (you will still slide down slowly)
- when holding z, use up and down arrows to climb

# Win condition
- reach the right side of the map
